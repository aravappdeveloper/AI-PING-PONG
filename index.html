AI PING PONG GAME





Developed By Arav Jain in 2022 | Arav's Teacher: Darshana S | Game by ByjusFutureSchool Team

How To Play

  1. Keep your laptop screen right-side-up
  2. Move yourself about 3-4 feet away from your webcam
  3. When you move your right wrist in an ascending/descending manner, a red dot should appear on it
  4. Now, press the play button. Now move back 3-4 feet, again
  5. The red paddle will move in sync with your wrist
